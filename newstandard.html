About Us


Established in 2049 BS, the New Standard is a private-spirited institution that makes distinctive contributions to society in research, learning. We strive for quality education at affordable cost by using recent pedagogies so as to attain academic excellence.

Our mission is to provide a safe, disciplined learning environment that empowers all students to develop their full potential. We feel strongly about helping to build leaders that have the ability to succeed in whatever endeavor they undertake.
